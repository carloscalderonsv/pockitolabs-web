Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Pockito Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates the SplitSmart mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use SplitSmart.

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Name and email address (when you sign up)
  • Profile photo (optional, or imported from Google/Apple)
  • Phone number (optional)

Expense Data

  • Expense descriptions, amounts, categories, and dates
  • Group names and member information
  • Receipt photos you choose to scan
  • Notes attached to expenses

Usage Data

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device type and operating system
  • Crash reports and error logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the SplitSmart service
  • To process your expenses and split calculations
  • To power Penny AI features and personalized insights
  • To send important account notifications
  • To process subscription payments via the App Store or Google Play
  • To respond to support requests

3. AI Features (Penny)

SplitSmart uses Claude AI (by Anthropic) to power Penny, our AI assistant. When you interact with Penny, your messages and relevant expense context are sent to Anthropic's API to generate responses. Anthropic's privacy policy applies to this data. We do not store your Penny conversations beyond your active session.

Receipt scanning uses Claude AI to extract expense information from photos. Receipt images are sent to Anthropic's API for processing and are not stored by us after extraction.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — our database and authentication provider
  • Anthropic — to power AI features
  • RevenueCat — to manage subscriptions
  • Apple / Google — for authentication and payment processing

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption and security practices to protect your data. Your data is stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure. We use HTTPS for all data transmission.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us at hello@pockitolabs.com.

7. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your expense data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications

8. Children's Privacy

SplitSmart is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by email. Continued use of SplitSmart after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us
