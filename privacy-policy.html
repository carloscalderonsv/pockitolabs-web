Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

Pockito Labs ("we", "our", or "us") operates multiple applications and services. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information across all Pockito Labs products, including VaultX, SplitSmart, and Luno.

1. Applications Covered

This policy applies to all Pockito Labs applications:

  • VaultX — Password manager with zero-knowledge encryption
  • SplitSmart — Bill-splitting and expense tracking app
  • Luno — LGBTQ+ dating and social connection platform

2. Information We Collect

Account Information (All Apps)

  • Name and email address when you sign up
  • Profile photo (optional, or imported from Google/Apple sign-in)
  • Phone number (optional, required for certain features)
  • Authentication credentials (encrypted)

VaultX-Specific Data

  • Encrypted password vault data (stored with zero-knowledge encryption)
  • Master password hash (never stored in plain text)
  • Vault metadata (item count, last sync time)
  • Device information for sync and security features
  • Security audit logs (login attempts, vault access)

SplitSmart-Specific Data

  • Expense descriptions, amounts, categories, and dates
  • Group names and member information
  • Receipt photos you choose to scan
  • Notes attached to expenses
  • Payment settlement history

Luno-Specific Data

  • Profile information (bio, photos, preferences)
  • Gender identity and sexual orientation
  • Age and location (city-level only)
  • Matching preferences and filters
  • Conversation messages (encrypted end-to-end)
  • Profile verification data (for safety features)

Usage Data (All Apps)

  • App usage patterns and feature interactions
  • Device type, operating system, and app version
  • Crash reports and error logs
  • IP address and general location (country/region)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our services
  • To power AI features (Penny in SplitSmart, CipherAI in VaultX, matching in Luno)
  • To enable core functionality specific to each app
  • To send important account notifications and security alerts
  • To process subscription payments via App Store or Google Play
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. AI Features and Third-Party Processing

Our apps use Claude AI (by Anthropic) to power various features:

  • SplitSmart — Penny AI assistant and receipt scanning. Your messages and expense context are sent to Anthropic's API. We do not store conversation history beyond your active session.
  • VaultX — CipherAI security assistant (opt-in). Queries and relevant security context are sent to Anthropic's API. Passwords are never sent to AI services.
  • Luno — AI-powered matching and conversation starters. Profile data and preferences are processed locally; only anonymized matching signals are sent to Anthropic's API.

Anthropic's privacy policy applies to data processed through their API. Images sent for receipt scanning are not stored after processing.

5. Zero-Knowledge Encryption (VaultX)

VaultX uses zero-knowledge encryption, meaning:

  • Your master password never leaves your device
  • All vault data is encrypted locally using AES-256-GCM before sync
  • We cannot decrypt your vault data, even if compelled by law
  • Only you can access your passwords — losing your master password means permanent data loss

6. End-to-End Encryption (Luno)

Luno conversations are end-to-end encrypted:

  • Messages are encrypted on your device before transmission
  • Only you and your conversation partner can read messages
  • We cannot access message contents, even for moderation purposes
  • Reported messages may be decrypted by the reporter for safety review

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication provider (all apps)
  • Anthropic — AI processing (Claude API)
  • RevenueCat — Subscription management (SplitSmart, VaultX, Luno)
  • Apple / Google — Authentication and payment processing
  • Vercel — API infrastructure and hosting

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect our rights, or to prevent harm.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in El Salvador and other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, complying with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable data protection laws.

9. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data
  • HTTPS/TLS for all data transmission
  • Secure authentication with OAuth 2.0 and biometric options
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Automatic logout and session management

No system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but maintain commercially reasonable safeguards.

10. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide services. Specific retention periods:

  • Account data — Retained until account deletion
  • VaultX encrypted vaults — Retained until account deletion; cannot be recovered after deletion
  • SplitSmart expenses — Retained until manual deletion or account deletion
  • Luno messages — Retained until manual deletion or account deletion
  • Logs and analytics — Retained for 90 days
  • Backup copies — Deleted within 30 days of account deletion

11. Your Rights

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export — Download your data in portable formats (JSON, CSV)

  • Restrict processing — Limit how we use your data

GDPR (European Users): You have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to lodge a complaint with your data protection authority.

CCPA (California Users): You have the right to know what personal information we collect, delete your information, and opt out of sale (we do not sell data).

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@pockitolabs.com.

12. Children's Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect data from children. Luno requires users to be 18+. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

13. Cookies and Tracking

  • No advertising cookies — We do not use advertising or third-party tracking
